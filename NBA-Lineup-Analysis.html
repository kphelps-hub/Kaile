NBA Lineup Analysis

This project seek to provide tools for looking at how teams lineups change between the Playoffs and the Regular Season. I particularly focused on looking at whether teams preferred to play smaller during the playoffs and how that trend has changed overtime.

Project Details

This project pulled data from pbpstats.com. I used Python to pull the data and do the initial cleaning steps. I then proceeded to visualize the data in 4 different worksheets in Tableau.

  1. Undersized Pct Over Time: a visualization of how teams played undersized players over time
  2. Undersized Scatter Plot: an interactive visualization to see teams that played significantly differently in the regular season vs the playoffs
  3. Undersized Playoff to Regular Season Ratio: a visualization broken down by position comparing the frequency of playing undersized in the playoffs
  4. Team Lineup Distribution: a tool to look at how individual teams played players at different positions
